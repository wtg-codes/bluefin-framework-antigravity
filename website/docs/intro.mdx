# Introduction

Welcome to the **Bluefin Framework wtgOS** documentation.

This project is a highly specialized "spin" of **Bluefin-DX**, custom-built for the **Framework Laptop 13** and advanced **Agentic AI workflows**.

## What is wtgOS?

wtgOS is designed for developers who want the stability and security of an immutable OS, paired with a high-performance, isolated environment for AI development.

### The Foundation: Bluefin-DX

We build upon [Bluefin-DX](https://projectbluefin.io), inheriting:

- **Atomic Updates:** Never worry about a broken system update.
- **Cloud-Native Tooling:** Docker, Podman, and Kind ready out of the box.
- **Ubuntu Compatibility:** Homebrew and Distrobox provide a familiar ecosystem.

### The Layer: wtgOS Additions

On top of the Bluefin base, we add:

- **Framework 13 Optimization:** Specific kernel tuning for Ryzen AI 300 series.
- **Quarantined AI:** A dedicated, declarative Distrobox for AI agents with ROCm passthrough.
- **TDI Validation:** Test-Driven Infrastructure ensures your OS is always verified.

## Getting Started

Ready to dive in? Check out the [Installation Guide](./installation) to get your Framework 13 running wtgOS.
